Step 01 · Email Waterfall
LeadMagic — Primary Email Finder
LeadMagic API Built-in Verification

Hits /profile-find by LinkedIn URL. Returns email with native verification — valid, catch_all, or invalid. Also returns company name, domain, and employee count. If a valid or catch-all email is found, AI Ark is skipped entirely.

email email_status company_name company_domain employee_count
Step 01 (Fallback) · Email Waterfall
AI Ark — Email Fallback
AI Ark API Catch-All Aware
↺ Only runs if LeadMagic returned no email

Handles catch-all domains and bounce detection natively. Confidence maps to email status: high→valid, medium→catch_all, low→invalid. If neither API finds an email, enrichment continues with email_status: not_found.

email email_source email_status
